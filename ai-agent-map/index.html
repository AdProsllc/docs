CAB Analyst Agents

AI Analyst for Creative Strategists — Full Agent Decomposition

Replace High Week 1–2
Creative Scorecard Agent
Auto-generates weekly and bi-weekly creative performance scorecards per client. Pulls from Meta Ads API, Google Ads API, Northbeam/Triple Whale, and Motion. Classifies ads into the 6 performance stages using the Ad Pros intelligence engine.
⌄

Meta Ads API, Google Ads API, Northbeam, Triple Whale, Motion

Branded scorecard per client — Testing, Winner, Missed Breakthrough, False Breakthrough, Breakthrough, Loser

Weekly + bi-weekly scheduled run

Manual analyst scorecard work (~6–10 hrs/week per client)

Replace High Week 2–4
Competitor Intel Agent
Continuously monitors competitor ad libraries via Meta Ad Library API + Apify scrapers. Identifies winning angles, emerging formats, new hooks, and spend patterns. Feeds structured briefs to the Creative Strategist with actionable competitor insights.
⌄

Meta Ad Library API, Apify scrapers, TikTok Creative Center

Weekly competitor brief per client — top angles, hooks, creative formats, spend signals

Daily crawl, weekly digest delivery

Manual competitor research sessions (~3–5 hrs/week per strategist)

Augment High Week 3–5
Creative Testing Roadmap Agent
Given performance data + competitor intel, generates a prioritized creative testing roadmap. Suggests concept-level tests, variant iterations, and scaling strategies. Outputs a structured testing plan the Creative Strategist can approve or modify.
⌄

Scorecard output, competitor brief, historical test results

Prioritized testing roadmap — concepts, variants, hypotheses, success metrics

Post-scorecard run, bi-weekly

Creative Strategist testing ideation — cuts planning time by ~70%

Replace Medium Week 4–6
QBR Deck Builder Agent
Auto-generates quarterly business review slide decks from client data. Pulls performance trends, creative highlights, testing results, and forward-looking recommendations into a branded PPTX template.
⌄

90-day performance data, scorecard history, testing outcomes

Branded PPTX — trends, wins, testing recap, Q+1 recommendations

Quarterly, 2 weeks before QBR date

Manual deck builds (~8–12 hrs per client per quarter)

Augment High Week 2–6 (parallel)
KPI Hierarchy & Metrics Agent
Defines, calculates, and maintains creative KPIs across the full hierarchy — in-platform metrics, MTA (Northbeam/Triple Whale), incrementality, and MMM (Prescient/Haus). Auto-adjusts metrics by channel.
⌄

All ad platform APIs, Northbeam, Triple Whale, Prescient, Haus

Unified KPI framework per client — channel-adjusted, updated in real-time

Continuous sync + weekly reconciliation

Analyst and strategist reporting — single source of truth for all performance data

Replace Medium Week 5–7
Avatar & Audience Analysis Agent
Connects to Faraday for comprehensive avatar analysis. Maps demographic and psychographic data to offer strategy. Generates audience profiles with marketing recommendations per client segment.
⌄

Faraday, Meta audience insights, client CRM data

Avatar profiles — demographics, psychographics, offer angles, messaging recommendations per segment

New client onboarding + quarterly refresh

Manual audience research and persona building (~4–6 hrs per engagement)

Replace Medium Week 1–3 (parallel)
Slack Notification & Alerting Agent
Trigger-based and scheduled Slack notifications for creative performance changes, budget pacing, winning/losing ad alerts, and deadline reminders. Keeps the Growth team appraised in real-time.
⌄

Performance drops, winner scaling signals, budget pacing, creative fatigue, deadline reminders

Real-time Slack messages to relevant channels with context and recommended action

Threshold-based + scheduled daily digest

Manual monitoring and ad-hoc team updates

Augment Medium Week 6–8
Creative Spend Forecasting Agent
Forecasts creative spend and budgets based on historical performance curves, testing velocity, and scaling patterns. Provides scenario modeling for different spend levels.
⌄

Historical spend data, performance curves, testing cadence, scaling history

3-scenario forecast (conservative/base/aggressive) with recommended spend allocation

Monthly + on-demand for client conversations

Media planning and client budget conversations — adds data-backed confidence

Replace High Week 3–6
Dashboard Factory Agent
Auto-creates and maintains client dashboards in Looker Studio. Handles data connections, adds new pages/metrics on request, and ensures dashboards stay live and updated without manual intervention.
⌄

Looker Studio, all ad platform APIs, Northbeam, Triple Whale, Google Sheets

Always-live client dashboards — auto-updated, self-healing on data failures

New client setup + ongoing maintenance + on-demand page additions

Manual dashboard builds and maintenance (~3–5 hrs/month per client)

Augment Medium Week 7–9
CAB Workshop Prep Agent
Pre-builds workshop materials by synthesizing all agent outputs into a structured workshop deck. Includes performance highlights, competitive intel, testing opportunities, and recommended discussion topics per client tier (Foundation, Accelerator, Breakthrough).
⌄

All agent outputs — scorecard, competitor brief, testing roadmap, KPI report

Tiered workshop deck with talking points, recommended decisions, and open questions

72 hours before each CAB workshop

Strategist workshop prep — reduces prep time from 4 hrs to ~30 min review

Core Org Agents

Internal operations, client management, and growth infrastructure

Replace High Week 1–3
Client Onboarding Agent
Automates the full client onboarding sequence — intake form processing, account access requests, tool provisioning, kickoff deck generation, and first-week task assignment across the team.
⌄

Contract signed / new client record created in CRM

Onboarding checklist, kickoff deck, Slack channel, access provisioning requests

Manual onboarding coordination (~6–10 hrs per new client)

HubSpot, Slack, Google Drive, Notion

Augment High Week 2–4
Revenue & Pipeline Intelligence Agent
Monitors pipeline health, flags at-risk accounts, surfaces upsell signals, and generates weekly revenue summaries for leadership. Tracks MRR, churn risk, and expansion opportunities across the client base.
⌄

HubSpot CRM, billing system, client health scores

Weekly pipeline report, at-risk alerts, upsell recommendations for AMs

Weekly + threshold alerts for churn signals

Leadership revenue reviews and AM account planning

Replace Medium Week 3–5
Meeting Prep & Recap Agent
Pulls relevant context before every client call — recent performance, open action items, last meeting notes — and generates a pre-read brief. Post-call, summarizes the meeting with action items auto-assigned in project management tools.
⌄

Google Calendar, Zoom/Fireflies, ClickUp, Notion, Slack

Pre-read brief (5 min before call) + post-call summary with action items

Calendar event creation / meeting end

Manual meeting prep and note-taking (~1–2 hrs per call)

Augment High Week 4–6
Proposal & Scope Builder Agent
Generates customized proposals and scope-of-work documents from a discovery call transcript or intake form. Pulls in relevant case studies, pricing tiers, and service descriptions to produce a ready-to-send proposal in under 10 minutes.
⌄

Discovery call transcript or intake form, prospect details, target tier

Branded proposal PDF + SOW with pricing, deliverables, and timeline

Post-discovery call or manual trigger by sales/AM

Sales and AM proposal process — from 4+ hrs to ~15 min review-and-send

Replace Medium Week 5–7
Hiring & Talent Pipeline Agent
Manages job post distribution, screens inbound applications against defined criteria, scores candidates, schedules first-round interviews, and maintains a warm pipeline of qualified candidates for recurring roles.
⌄

Ashby/Greenhouse, LinkedIn, job boards, Google Calendar

Scored applicant shortlist, automated outreach, interview schedules

New job posting created or pipeline falls below threshold

Manual application review and scheduling coordination (~5–8 hrs per open role/week)

Augment Medium Week 6–8
Internal Knowledge & SOPs Agent
Maintains a searchable, always-current internal knowledge base. Answers team questions about processes, client history, and playbooks. Auto-updates SOPs when processes change and flags outdated documentation for review.
⌄

Notion, Google Drive, Slack (slash command interface)

Instant answers to process questions, flagged outdated SOPs, updated documentation

Slack query, document update, or scheduled monthly audit

Onboarding new hires and reducing repeated Slack questions to team leads

Replace Medium Week 7–9
Finance & Billing Ops Agent
Automates invoice generation, tracks payment status, sends follow-up sequences for overdue accounts, and reconciles monthly billing against active contracts. Surfaces discrepancies and flags contract renewals 60 days out.
⌄

QuickBooks/Stripe, HubSpot, contract management tool

Auto-generated invoices, payment status reports, renewal alerts, overdue follow-up sequences

Monthly billing cycle + threshold-based payment alerts

Manual billing coordination and accounts receivable follow-up (~4–6 hrs/month)

Augment Medium Week 8–10
Content & Thought Leadership Agent
Repurposes internal insights, client wins, and team expertise into LinkedIn posts, newsletter content, and case study drafts. Maintains a publishing calendar and queues content for team review before posting.
⌄

Client results, internal Slack highlights, meeting transcripts, industry news

Draft LinkedIn posts, newsletter sections, case study outlines — queued for human approval

Weekly content calendar + on-demand from notable client wins

Marketing and leadership content presence without adding content headcount
