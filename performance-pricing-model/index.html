How the model works

Three components stack to align fees with results.
01 — FOUNDATION

Base + Variable Fee

$10,000 flat retainer plus a tiered media buying fee on spend (4% on $62.5K–$500K, 3% on $500K–$1M, declining at scale).

Base retainer$10,000 Variable @ $300K$9,500 Pre-multiplier total$19,500
02 — MULTIPLIER

CPA Performance

Both base and variable fees scale by a multiplier driven by actual CPA vs. the $60 target. Capped at 1.50× (great), floored at 0.70× (poor).

$30 CPA1.50× $60 CPA (target)1.00× $90 CPA0.70×
03 — UNLOCK

Spend Bonus

An additional % of monthly spend, but only if CPA is at or below $60. Rewards scaling efficiently — bigger budgets at target = bigger bonus.

$60 CPA0.25% of spend $45 CPA0.50% of spend $30 CPA0.75% of spend
!

Why this works for both sides. At target, the total fee is $20,250 — only modestly higher than the original $13,860 flat-fee proposal at this spend level. The upside ($24K at $50 CPA) and downside ($16.6K at $75 CPA) put real money on the line in both directions, which signals confidence and creates aligned incentives. The deeper upside compared to downside reflects that hitting $60 CPA at scale is genuine value created, not just operational throughput.
