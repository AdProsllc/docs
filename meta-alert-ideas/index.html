Meta Alert Ideas for Ecommerce Media Buyers

50 alert concepts across 11 categories — input list for the Workflow Factory

Context

Working list of automated alert ideas for the growth team — Meta only, lean spec. Triggered by the Win Pro weekly report thread (Apr 27 wk) where Kevin pushed back on the campaign performance table and said the more useful direction is "automated reports that tie campaign performance trends to actions taken."

Each alert is a candidate for the master menu Nehal proposed: media buyers and creative team subscribe to the alerts they actually want to see. Thresholds left soft — per-brand config (Win Pro, 47skin, Broya Halal, Luhxe etc.) plugs in at deployment.
