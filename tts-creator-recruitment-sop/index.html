Source Channels (Priority Order)

Layer these channels from warmest to coldest. Start with existing relationships and expand outward.

1 Luhxe's Existing Organic Creators

Search TikTok for existing Luhxe UGC — anyone who has already tagged or mentioned the brand. These are the warmest leads with the highest conversion rate. Pull their profiles, check if they're TTS-enabled, and reach out directly.

Expected yield: 10–30 warm leads. Conversion rate 50–70% (they already love the product).

2 TikTok Shop Affiliate Marketplace

Use TikTok's built-in affiliate marketplace to find creators already selling in the travel, fashion, and lifestyle categories. Filter by GMV, follower count, and engagement rate. Invite directly through the platform.

3 TikTok Creator Marketplace (TTCM)

Official TikTok platform for brand–creator partnerships. Advanced filters for audience demographics, content performance, and engagement metrics. Send collaboration invitations directly.

4 Competitor Creator Poaching via Analytics Tools

Multiple analytics platforms track which creators are promoting which products on TikTok Shop. Use these to identify proven performers selling competitor products (Away, Béis, Monos, July, Samsonite).

5 Instagram / YouTube Cross-Platform Sourcing

Many TikTok creators also post on Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts. Search travel and packing content on those platforms, find creators with TikTok presence, and cross-reference.

6 Scraping Tools for Email Extraction

Use IGLeads or similar tools to bulk-scrape emails from TikTok/Instagram bios. Always verify emails with NeverBounce or ZeroBounce before sending to maintain deliverability.

7 Off-Platform Communities

Recruit from Discord servers, Facebook Groups, and Reddit communities focused on TikTok Shop selling, UGC creation, and influencer marketing. These creators are actively looking for brand deals.

YouTube Meta Playbook

Based on strategies being actively taught by e-commerce experts (Ecom Mastery, Tiktokwiz, Ashley Wright, Mina Elias) on YouTube. The meta has shifted from manual one-by-one outreach to automated, data-driven, competitor-focused strategies.

Strategy 1: Competitor Swipe

The highest-signal approach. Instead of cold-sourcing unknown creators, target people already proven to sell products in your category.

Workflow: Open Kalodata/FastMoss/EchoTik → search competitor products (Away, Béis, Monos, July, Samsonite) → sort by creator GMV → export top performers → pitch with money-driven angle.

Why it works: These creators already know how to sell travel gear on TikTok. They have the audience, the hooks, and the production skills. You're not teaching them — you're redirecting them with a better offer.

Luhxe angle: "You're already selling travel bags and earning commission-only. We guarantee $150/mo + 3% of ad spend. Most of our creators earn $500–$2,000/mo."

Strategy 2: Mass Automation

Scale outreach to thousands of creators per day using automation tools. The volume play — lower conversion per message, but raw numbers win.

Strategy 3: Commission-Only (Strategic Contrast)

Most YouTube educators teach a pure commission model: 15–20% commission, no base pay, "zero upfront cost for the brand." This is the dominant meta for TTS recruitment.

Why Luhxe should NOT do this: The guaranteed $150/month base is the single biggest differentiator. Every other brand is doing commission-only. The base pay is what makes your outreach message different from the 50 other DMs a creator gets this week.

Positioning: Frame the $150 as "we invest in our creators" — it signals the brand is serious, well-funded, and values the relationship. Commission-only programs have 3–5x higher churn because creators feel replaceable.

Strategy 4: Community Harvesting

Instead of finding creators one by one, tap into existing communities where creators congregate.

Customer → Creator Pipeline: Add "Become a Luhxe Creator" CTA to luhxe.com, order confirmation emails, and pack-in cards. Customers who already love the product make the most authentic content.

Discord / Facebook Groups: Join and recruit from groups focused on TikTok Shop selling, UGC creation, and side hustle income. Post the opportunity (with guaranteed pay headline) and let creators come to you.

Creator Marketplaces: Post campaigns on Insense, Collabstr, and TikTok Creator Marketplace. These are inbound channels — creators apply to you, flipping the outreach dynamic.
