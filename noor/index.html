08 · Do's & Don'ts

Keep Noor, Noor.

✓ Do

  • Lock the iso angle. Don't freestyle.
  • Always ground her with a drop shadow.
  • Brand yellow face. Halo is the only other yellow.
  • Confident, data-specific copy. No "Hi there!"
  • Navy background by default.

× Don't

  • Anti-alias her. Keep it pixelated.
  • Add eyelashes or lipstick — hair + mouth do the work.
  • Run more than one Noor on a screen.
  • Place her over photos or non-brand gradients.
  • Make her apologize, giggle, or use emoji.
