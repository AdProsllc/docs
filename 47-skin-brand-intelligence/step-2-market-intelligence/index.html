2 — Customer Voice & Jobs to Be Done

Framework: Christensen's Jobs to Be Done + Moesta's Four Forces of Progress. Customers don't buy skincare — they hire it to make progress. Understanding the functional, emotional, and social job unlocks the creative brief.
Functional Job
"Help me clear my (or my child's) skin of blemishes and stop new ones forming — without harsh chemicals, dryness, or irritation."
Emotional Job
"Give me (or my child) back the confidence to leave the house, face people, and not feel defined by their skin."
Social Job
"Let me be a parent who found the answer — the one who solved this problem when nothing else worked. Give me the win."

4 — Market Trends & Innovation Signals

01
Adult Breakout Awareness Explosion
Adult breakouts are the category's fastest-growing segment (14.1% CAGR). 50% of women in their 20s and 35% in their 30s deal with hormonal breakouts. Most brands still speak to teens. This is the white space — and 47 Skin's Silver Chitoderm® narrative fits perfectly for a more sophisticated adult audience.
02
Ingredient Fatigue → Synergistic Formulas
Single-hero ingredient trend (niacinamide, vitamin C) is fading. Consumers now want synergistic blends. Silver Chitoderm® is a proprietary blend — positioned perfectly as a "complete solution," not another single-ingredient trend product.
03
Personalization & Quiz-Based Shopping
Curology and others have proven that quiz-based, "skin assessment" funnels convert significantly better for blemish products. 47 Skin has a UK quiz ("Find Out What Your Skin Is Telling You") — not yet live for US. This is a clear execution gap vs. the market trend.
04
Social Proof as the Primary Purchase Driver
TikTok and Instagram have normalized "skin journey" content. Consumers trust peer reviews and before/afters over brand claims. The Mighty Patch "gunk reveal" went viral. 47 Skin's real-results, no-filter ethos is perfectly aligned with this trend — but US content volume is currently too low.
05
Destigmatization of Breakouts
Brands like Hero Cosmetics shifted breakout care "from medicine cabinet to makeup bag." Cultural acceptance of visible skin struggles is growing. However, the emotional pain of breakouts is still very real — especially for teens and their parents. This dual reality is the creative opportunity: acknowledge the struggle, celebrate the fix.
06
Natural / Clinically-Backed Hybrid
Consumers want products that are simultaneously natural AND proven. They're skeptical of harsh chemicals but also skeptical of unproven "natural" claims. Silver Chitoderm® sits in the sweet spot — derived from natural sources (shellfish shells), clinically proven, dermatologist approved.
