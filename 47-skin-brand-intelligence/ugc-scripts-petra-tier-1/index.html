47 Skin — UGC Creator Scripts

Protective Parent Petra · Tier 1 Angles · US Meta + TikTok · March 2026
Ad Pros Brand Intelligence 5 Scripts · Step 6 Tier 1 30–45 sec

📋 How to use these scripts

  • Scripts are guides, not teleprompters
  • Speak naturally in your own voice
  • Improvise around the structure — ad-libs welcome
  • Slight stumbles = authenticity signal, keep them
  • Film multiple takes, deliver lines differently each time

🎬 Production notes

  • Film at home — kitchen, bathroom or living room
  • Natural light preferred. No ring light required
  • Phone-held or propped is fine — no studio setup
  • Minimal make-up, casual clothes, real environment
  • Hold the 47 Skin serum bottle on camera at the close
  • Vertical 9:16 format (1080×1920px)

⚠️ Compliance — must follow

  • Say "Silver Chitoderm®" — never just Silver or Chitoderm
  • Say "clinically proven to kill 100% of bacteria" — not just "kills bacteria"
  • Use: blemishes, spots, breakouts, bad skin — NOT "acne" as a brand claim
  • Meta: "scar repair" language is fine
  • TikTok: say "blemish marks" instead of "scar repair" — platform restriction
  • Do NOT say you were paid. Do NOT claim to cure a medical condition.

Angle 47SK-S6-01 · Inspirational · Transformation Story
The Mom Who Finally Found It — After Trying Everything
🎯 Petra — Mom of teen 13–18 ⏱ 30–40 sec 📍 Talking head at home 🎬 TOF Cold Traffic 📱 Meta + TikTok
8.85 / 10
🎣 Hook — First 3 seconds (scroll stopper)
"If your teen's skin has stopped responding to everything you've tried — you need to watch this."
Deliver directly to camera. Slight lean forward. No smile yet — this is empathy first.
📝 Full Script
[Seated casually — couch or kitchen counter. Earnest, warm. Direct eye contact with camera throughout.] "I spent over a year trying products for my daughter's skin. The gentle stuff. The clinical stuff. The things that were all over TikTok. None of it worked. She started pulling out of photos. Started saying she didn't want to go places. I just felt helpless. Then someone in a mom group mentioned 47 Skin. I was skeptical — we'd been let down so many times before. But it has this unique ingredient called Silver Chitoderm® — clinically proven to kill 100% of the bacteria that actually cause breakouts. Not treat the surface. Kill what's causing it. Week one — visible difference. Week three — she asked to go to a birthday party. [Pause. Smile. Hold product up naturally.] I wish I had found this first." [Look back at camera, warm and direct.] "Link in bio — they have a trial size so you can try it without the risk."
🎬 Creator Direction
Performance notes
  • Start with genuine empathy — no smile until the resolution moment
  • The "pulled out of photos" line should land quietly, not dramatically
  • Let the "week one, week three" beat have a natural pause between them
  • The smile + product reveal at the end should feel like relief, not triumph
  • "I wish I had found this first" is the emotional close — don't rush it
Casting & setting notes
  • Mom aged 35–50, natural, no heavy styling
  • Optional: teen visible briefly in background (does not need to speak)
  • Warm home setting — lived-in, not staged
  • Natural morning or window light preferred
  • Product: 47 Skin Anti-Blemish & Scar Repair Serum — hold at chest height
✅ Compliance Check
✓ Silver Chitoderm® — correct ✓ "Clinically proven to kill 100% of bacteria" — correct ✓ No medical condition claims ✓ "Breakouts" not "acne" — correct ✓ Meta safe — no TikTok-restricted language ✓ Trial size CTA — low commitment ask

Angle 47SK-S6-02 · UGC · Raw Testimonial
I Tried Everything for My Daughter's Skin — This Is the Only Thing That Worked
🎯 Petra — Mom of teen 13–18 ⏱ 25–35 sec 📍 Phone-held, self-filmed 🎬 TOF Cold Traffic 📱 Meta + TikTok
8.85 / 10
🎣 Hook — First 3 seconds
[Hold up fingers, start counting off] "Nine products. Two dermatologist appointments. Zero results."
Deadpan delivery. Count the fingers visually. No context given yet — let the viewer fill in the blank and hook themselves.
📝 Full Script
[Phone held at eye level or slightly below. Casual — kitchen, bathroom, wherever feels natural. Self-film energy. Slight camera shake is fine.] "Nine products. Two dermatologist appointments. Zero results. My daughter and I have been through everything for her skin. Prescription stuff. Expensive serums. The things every skincare page keeps posting about. Every time we thought — maybe this one — it wasn't. Until 47 Skin. They have this serum with Silver Chitoderm® — I'd never heard of it before either. It's a unique formula that's clinically proven to kill 100% of the bacteria that cause breakouts. At the source. Not the surface. First week — her skin calmed down. Second week — breakouts clearing. Third week — [Small pause. Lower voice slightly, genuine.] — she looks in the mirror and smiles now. If you're stuck in the same cycle I was in — [Hold up product. Direct look at camera.] They have a trial size. Start there. I'm not saying it didn't work."
🎬 Creator Direction
Performance notes
  • The hook should be slightly flat/exhausted — not angry, just tired of trying
  • "I'd never heard of it before either" — this is important, say it naturally. It gives permission to not know the brand.
  • The week-by-week beat should build like you're re-living it
  • "She looks in the mirror and smiles now" — pause here. Let it land.
  • "I'm not saying it didn't work" = understated confidence. Don't over-deliver it.
Format notes
  • This is the most platform-native format — feels like a voice note to a friend
  • No ring light. Natural or bathroom light is ideal
  • Phone slightly wobbling is fine — too steady looks produced
  • Product at the end: hold it naturally, not like a brand reveal
  • This script works well as a Reel cover with text overlay of the hook line

✓ Silver Chitoderm® — correct ✓ "Clinically proven" qualifier present ✓ "Breakouts" not "acne" — correct ✓ No paid endorsement language ✓ Meta + TikTok safe

Angle 47SK-S6-03 · Inspirational · Emotional Withdrawal Arc
Watching My Teen Withdraw Because of Their Skin Broke My Heart. Then This Happened.
🎯 Petra — Mom of teen 13–18 ⏱ 35–45 sec 📍 Seated at home, vulnerable tone 🎬 TOF Cold Traffic 📱 Meta primary
8.67 / 10
🎣 Hook — First 3 seconds
"The moment my daughter refused to be in the family photo — that was when I knew I had to do something different."
Quiet, direct. No build-up. Drop straight into the emotional scene. This hook works on recognition — any parent who has experienced this will stop immediately.
📝 Full Script
[Seated. Could be at a kitchen table with a coffee cup visible. Slightly more intimate framing. Direct eye contact — like you're talking to a friend who gets it.] "The moment my daughter refused to be in the family photo — that was when I knew I had to do something different. She'd been dealing with her skin for almost two years. And I watched her go from this confident, outgoing kid — to someone who wouldn't leave the house without full coverage. Who sat out of photos. Who started saying she just didn't feel like going to things anymore. I tried everything I could find. Nothing stuck. A friend told me about 47 Skin. Their serum has Silver Chitoderm® — it's a unique formula, clinically proven to kill 100% of the bacteria that cause breakouts. Not treat what you can see. Target what's actually causing it. Within three weeks her skin was clearing. [Softer. Slight smile creeping in.] But the moment I knew it was really working? She came downstairs one morning without any makeup on. [Pause. Hold product up quietly.] Link in bio. If you've been where I was — it's worth trying."
🎬 Creator Direction
Performance notes
  • This is the most emotional of the five scripts — commit to it fully
  • "Confident, outgoing kid" should sound like you're remembering who she was before
  • "Sat out of photos / didn't feel like going to things" — say this like it's still a little painful to remember
  • The "came downstairs without makeup" reveal is the payoff — slow down here
  • The CTA at the end should feel like a quiet recommendation, not a sell
Optional enhancements
  • If you have a teen who's willing: brief appearance in background at the end (not required)
  • A single photo of the product placed naturally in frame adds visual interest
  • This script works well as a slightly longer format (up to 45 sec) — don't rush the emotional beats to fit 30
  • Meta Reels and Facebook Feed are the primary placements for this emotional depth

Angle 47SK-S6-04 · Inspirational · Resolution-Led (Payoff First)
My Son's Confidence Is Back. I Haven't Seen Him Smile Like That in Years.
🎯 Petra — Mom of teen son ⏱ 30–40 sec 📍 Warm home setting 🎬 TOF Cold Traffic 📱 Meta + TikTok
8.67 / 10
🎣 Hook — First 3 seconds
[Big, genuine smile — real, not performed] "My son just asked to be in the family photo for the first time in two years. I need to tell you why."
Lead with the joy. Resolution-first structure. The viewer has to know what happened — this is the pattern-interrupt. Works especially well for parents of teen boys, an underserved creative segment.
📝 Full Script
[Warm. Genuine. The energy of someone who just had something great happen. Natural home setting — kitchen counter, living room. Could hold a cup of coffee.] "My son just asked to be in the family photo for the first time in two years. I need to tell you why. He'd been really self-conscious about his skin since about ninth grade. I tried everything — we did the prescription route, the gentle route, the expensive route. I was almost out of ideas. Someone recommended 47 Skin. I looked it up and honestly wasn't sure — but their serum has this unique ingredient called Silver Chitoderm® that's clinically proven to kill 100% of the bacteria that cause breakouts. At the source, not just the surface. [Tone shifts — quieter, more personal] Three weeks in — I could see a difference. Two months in — his skin was clear. And his whole energy changed. He's back out with his friends. He laughs differently. [Hold up product. Warm, direct.] That's not just a skincare result. That's my kid back. Trial size is in the link. It's worth every penny."
🎬 Creator Direction
Performance notes
  • Open with genuine joy — this script leads with the win, not the struggle
  • "I was almost out of ideas" — say this with a touch of exhaustion, not drama
  • "He laughs differently" — this is the standout line. Slow down. Let it sit.
  • "That's not just a skincare result. That's my kid back." — do not rush this close
  • Warm and grateful energy throughout — not triumphant, just relieved and happy
Why this script matters
  • Son/boy-focused content is underused in the current 47 Skin creative mix
  • Moms searching for teen boy solutions see almost no content that speaks to them specifically
  • Resolution-first format is a proven high-CTR structure — viewer must keep watching to get the explanation
  • Can be produced as static with a single strong pull quote alongside product

Angle 47SK-S6-05 · Storytelling · Problem → Fix → Outcome
When Nothing Else Worked for My Daughter's Skin, This Was the Only Thing That Actually Cleared It
🎯 Petra — Research-mode mom ⏱ 30–40 sec 📍 Seated at home 🎬 TOF–MOF 📱 Meta + TikTok
8.60 / 10
🎣 Hook — First 3 seconds
"The reason nothing you've tried for your teen's skin has worked yet — and what actually does."
Slightly more educational tone than the other scripts. Speaks directly to Petra's frustration and promises a resolution in the same sentence. Strong for research-mode viewers who are actively looking for answers.
📝 Full Script
[Seated, calm, slightly more explanatory. Like a knowledgeable friend who found something out and wants to share it. Not a lecture — a conversation.] "The reason nothing you've tried for your teen's skin has worked yet — and what actually does. Most skincare treats what you can see — the redness, the blemish. It doesn't go after what's actually causing it, which is bacteria. I found this out the hard way. Two years of trying things for my daughter's skin — the gentle stuff, the prescription stuff, the things that went viral. All of it treating symptoms. None of it touching the root cause. 47 Skin is different. Their serum has Silver Chitoderm® — a unique formula, clinically proven to kill 100% of the bacteria that cause breakouts. It goes to the source. And while it's working, it keeps skin hydrated — so it doesn't just strip everything and leave you worse off than before. [Week-by-week beat — deliberate, measured] She saw a difference in week one. Clear skin by week three. [Hold up product confidently.] If you're still in the loop of things that don't last — this is the one that broke the cycle for us. Trial kit is in the link."
🎬 Creator Direction
Performance notes
  • This is the most information-led of the five scripts — lean into the "knowledgeable friend" energy
  • "Bacteria" is the pivot word — say it with a bit of emphasis, like it's the answer
  • "None of it touching the root cause" — slight frustration okay here
  • "It doesn't just strip everything and leave you worse off" — this speaks directly to Petra's fear. Say it like you've experienced that disappointment.
  • Week-by-week beat: measured and factual — let the specificity do the work
Why this structure works
  • Problem → Fix → Outcome is the foundational direct-response structure
  • The "Fix" section does double duty: explains why other things didn't work AND why 47 Skin is different — this is the intellectual permission Petra needs to believe again
  • Suits a slightly warmer audience (retargeting or MOF) but strong enough for cold too
  • Works exceptionally well as a text-heavy static or carousel companion
✅ Compliance Check
✓ Silver Chitoderm® — correct ✓ "Clinically proven to kill 100% of bacteria" — correct ✓ "Breakouts" not "acne" ✓ No scar repair language — clean for TikTok and Meta ✓ Hydration claim is a factual product benefit — no compliance issue ✓ "Broke the cycle for us" — personal experience, not brand guarantee
