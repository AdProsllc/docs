The creative playbook is silver science + real skin + no filters. The acquisition path is Trial $25 (lowest friction, high-value entry point) → email upsell to 3-Step System. The following 60 days are scale — 20–30 new concepts per month, retargeting isolation, and subscription creative to close the LTV gap.

Decision Area 8
Compliance & Legal Guardrails
✓ Locked
Risk Tolerance
Moderate
Push the testimonial and transformation angle hard. Stay within cosmetic (not drug) framing at all times.
FDA Classification
Cosmetic — not drug
All claims must be cosmetic: "blemish" not "acne treatment." "Supports clearer-looking skin" not "clears acne." No drug efficacy claims.
Vocabulary — Use
Blemish · Breakout · Clearer-looking skin · Repair · Silver Chitoderm · Clinically tested · Transformation
All approved vocabulary from brand DNA. Use these terms in all ad copy and landing pages.
Vocabulary — Avoid
Cure acne · Eliminates acne · Acne treatment · 100% guaranteed · Pharmaceutical claims
These terms trigger drug claim classification. Never use in ads, LPs, or testimonials without disclaimer.
Testimonial Disclaimers
Required on all before/after content
"Results may vary. Individual results not guaranteed." FTC-compliant testimonial disclosures on all UGC and before/after ad formats.
MBG Terms
47-day money-back — terms required
Link to MBG terms on all guarantee-featuring ads and landing pages. Ensure Shopify returns flow is documented and operational.
Decision Area 9
Testing Matrix & Creative Iteration Strategy
✓ Locked
New Concepts / Month
20–30 (standard cadence)
4–6 new concepts per week. Systematic — not random. Each concept tests one variable at a time.
Iteration Ads / Month
10 iterations on winners
Top 3 performers get 3–4 variations each: hook change, CTA change, avatar-specific copy. Fast iteration beats starting over.
Kill Rule
72h — CAC at 2x target
Any ad exceeding 2x target CAC at 72 hours is paused. No emotional attachment to creative decisions.
Scale Rule
Day 5 — CAC at 0.8x target
Any ad achieving 0.8x target CAC at day 5 gets 20% budget increase per 48h until CAC degrades. Document the ceiling.
