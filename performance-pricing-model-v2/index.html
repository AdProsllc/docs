How the model works

Three components stack to align fees with results.
01 — FOUNDATION

Base + Variable Fee

$11,310 flat retainer (bundles creative strategy, creative production, and media buying base) plus a tiered media buying fee (4% on $62.5K–$500K, 3% on $500K–$1M, scaling down at higher volume).

Base retainer$11,310 Variable @ $300K$9,500 Pre-multiplier total$20,810
02 — MULTIPLIER

CPA Performance

Both fees scale by a multiplier driven by actual CPA vs. the $60 target. Capped at 1.50× (great), floored at 0.70× (poor).

$30 CPA1.50× $60 CPA (target)1.00× $90 CPA0.70×
03 — UNLOCK

Spend Bonus

An additional % of monthly spend that unlocks at target ($60 CPA or better) and grows with efficiency. Rewards scaling spend at high efficiency.

$60 CPA (target)0.25% of spend $45 CPA0.50% of spend $30 CPA0.75% of spend
!

How the incentives stack up. At $60 CPA the agency earns $21,560 — matching the original Proposal 2 investment level (the new $11,310 base bundles what was previously creative strategy, creative production, and media buying base). The upside (~$25.5K at $50 CPA) and downside (~$17.7K at $75 CPA) put real money on the line in both directions, which signals confidence and creates aligned incentives. The floor multiplier of 0.70× at $90+ CPA means the agency still earns enough to cover team time, but with a clear signal that something needs to change.
